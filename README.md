# Chocolate-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard
1.Project Title / Headline:

This project showcases a Power BI sales analytics dashboard designed to track business performance, identify top-selling products, and generate actionable insights from sales data

2.Short Description:

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze chocolate sales performance across products, countries, and salespersons. The report provides clear insights into revenue trends, top products, and overall business performance

3.Purpose:

->Monitor overall sales performance
->Identify top-performing products
->Analyze monthly revenue trends
->Compare country wise contribution
->Evaluate salesperson performance
->Support data-driven decision making

4.Tech Stack:

->Power BI
->Power Query (ETL & Data Cleaning)
->DAX (Measures & Calculations)
->Data Modeling

5.Data Source:

Sample Chocolate Sales dataset containing:
->Date
->Product
->Country
->Sales Person
->Sales Amount
->Boxes (Quantity)

->Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
->Top Products by Revenue
->Country Wise Sales Distribution
->Salesperson Performance Table
->Interactive Filters (Date Slicer)

For example, the dashboard highlights that a small group of products contributes a significant portion of total revenue, while certain countries consistently generate higher sales compared to others

..A sales manager wants to understand which chocolate products are generating the highest revenue and in which countries sales are strongest. Using this Power BI dashboard, the manager can quickly view monthly sales trends, identify top-performing products, compare country-wise performance, and evaluate salesperson contributions. This helps the business focus on high-demand products, improve sales strategies, and make data-driven decisions.
